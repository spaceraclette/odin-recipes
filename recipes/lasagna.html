Lasagna

Description

This is a really nice lasagna recipe, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • meat
  • milk
  • tomato
  • pasta
  • cheese

Preparation

  1. Sautée the meat
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Make béchamel sauce
  4. Layer ragu sauce with pasta sheets
  5. add cheese
  6. undo on 4 hundo
  7. bake to a crisp
